Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella heralded a new era in computing, declaring that we have entered the “middle innings of AI.” in his keynote address at Microsoft Ignite 2024. Nadella asserted that just as the launch of Windows 3.1, 32 years ago, defined Windows, AI is reshaping businesses and societies.

Nadella’s address centered around three core platforms:

1. Copilot:

Designed as the user interface for AI, Copilot is transforming how people work. It provides each employee with an AI assistant that knows them and their work, helping them unlock productivity, enhance their creativity, and save time.

Copilot Pages: Heralded as the first artifact of the AI age, Pages offer the ability to add interactive charts, tables, code blocks, math equations, and complex diagrams. Users can use Copilot directly on the page to iterate on the content and also control what happens on the page directly from a chat.

Deep Integration with Microsoft 365: Copilot is not just limited to Pages, but is integrated with the entirety of the Microsoft 365 system. In Teams, it can reason over all the past meetings and chats and transcripts and get you quickly up to speed. In Word, it can create drafts based on other Word documents, PowerPoints, PDFs, emails, and meetings. In PowerPoint, it allows users to enter a prompt of what they want to do and Copilot will generate a presentation outline. In Outlook, it helps prioritize your inbox. And in Excel, Copilot is a game-changer for data analysts, allowing users to perform complex analyses using natural language prompts.

Copilot Actions: These help users save time by automating repetitive everyday tasks. These Actions work across the entire M365 system and automate everything from asking for a status update from your team, compiling weekly reports, to scheduling emails, requesting feedback on a document.

Copilot Studio: This enables businesses to easily create their own Copilots or their own agents. This empowers them to develop custom solutions to automate processes and enhance efficiency.

2. Copilot Devices:

In the age of AI and cloud computing, devices are being transformed. Microsoft is bringing the power of AI in the cloud to the edge, creating one continuous distributed computing fabric.

Copilot+ PCs: These next-generation Windows PCs are designed to unleash the power of that distributed computing fabric between the cloud and the edge. With over 40 TOPS and NPUs, these PCs are enabling developers, such as Adobe and WhatsApp, to deliver breakthrough AI experiences.

Windows 365 Link: This simple, secure, and purpose-built device connects users directly to their productivity in the cloud, leaving no data or information behind on any device. It is admin-less, passwordless, and security configurations are enabled by default and cannot be turned off.

3. Copilot and AI Stack:

Microsoft is making every layer of its tech stack, from the apps it builds with Copilot, agents, and Copilot Studio, available for everyone to build their own Copilots and agents.

Infrastructure Layer: Azure, as the world’s computer, is constantly expanding, boasting over 60 data center regions, more than any other provider. Built with a focus on sustainability, Azure data centers are designed to minimize their carbon footprint.

Data Layer: Data is the foundation of AI. Microsoft Fabric brings together all your data and analytical workloads into one unified experience with OneLake. In Fabric, you can easily unify your data, no matter whether it lives in Azure, on-premises, or on Amazon or GCP.

Application Layer: Every application is becoming an AI application. Azure AI Foundry serves as a first-class app server for the AI age, unifying all of our models, tooling, safety, and monitoring solutions into a single experience. Foundry provides everything developers need to build and manage applications.

Nadella concluded his address by emphasizing Microsoft’s commitment to democratizing AI and making it accessible to everyone. The company has trained over 23 million people worldwide in AI and digital skills and is determined to help millions more learn how to use AI.

The vision shared at Microsoft Ignite 2024 reveals that AI is transcending mere technology and is becoming foundational to our society and the way we do business. By focusing on Copilot, devices, and the AI stack, Microsoft seeks to ensure that everyone benefits from this transformation and achieves more.
