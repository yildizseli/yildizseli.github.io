About me

I am (almost) a new graduate from Mechatronics Engineering at the University of Waterloo. I’ve spent the last five years taking an interdisciplinary approach to my engineering degree. With a wide range of mechanical, software, and electrical courses including digital controls, embedded programming, autonomous mobile robotics, and adaptive algorithms.

I’ve also had six co-op experiences to apply everything I’ve learned in school to industry. I’ve worked in nuclear energy, automotive manufacturing, robotics, and circuit design. Through all of these experiences I’ve learned so many technical skills, and even more importantly - how to learn quickly.

Beyond my technical pursuits, I value volunteering with FIRST Robotics and Women in STEM outreach programs. As well as rock climbing, back-country camping, sailing, and gymnastics.
